Animals with Organized Names

Animal names are fun, but you know what's even better? Animal names where all the letters are organized in order! Let me give you some examples.

Some Examples

Ant is an organized name, since, A is before N, and N is before T, in the alphabet.

Cat on the other hand is not an organized name, since, C is after A in the alphabet.

Your task is then to find the longest organized animal name. In case you wonder: You can safely ignore space.
